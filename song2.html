Song 2 - Die With a smile

This song is an album composed by "Lady gaga" and "Bruno Mars"

"Die With a Smile" is a collaborative single by American artists Lady Gaga and Bruno Mars, released on August 16, 2024. The song blends elements of soft rock and contemporary pop, reflecting themes of love and mortality. Its accompanying music video features a '70s-inspired aesthetic, with Mars on guitar and Gaga at the piano, performing on a retro television studio set. EW.COM Upon release, the track achieved significant commercial success, topping the Billboard Global 200 chart for nine consecutive weeks. EN.WIKIPEDIA.ORG It also reached number one on the Billboard Hot 100 in January 2025. EN.WIKIPEDIA.ORG At the 67th Annual Grammy Awards, "Die With a Smile" won Best Pop Duo/Group Performance, marking Gaga's third win in this category. EN.WIKIPEDIA.ORG The song was later included in Lady Gaga's album "Mayhem", released on March 7, 2025. EN.WIKIPEDIA.ORG
