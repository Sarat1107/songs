This song is from the movie "Game Changer". It is composed by S Thaman

"NaaNaa Hyraanaa" is a melodious Telugu song from the 2025 film Game Changer, featuring vocals by Karthik and Shreya Ghoshal. Composed by Thaman S with lyrics penned by Ramajogayya Sastry, the song showcases a romantic duet between the lead characters, portrayed by Ram Charan and Kiara Advani. The music video, choreographed by Bosco Martis, was filmed in the scenic landscapes of New Zealand, utilizing infrared camera technology to enhance its visual appeal.
