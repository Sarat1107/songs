"Inthandam" is a beautiful Telugu song from the 2023 movie *Sita Ramam*, composed by Vishal Chandrashekhar and sung by S. P. Charan. The song captures the essence of romance, beautifully portraying the emotions of love and admiration between the lead characters. With its soulful melody and poetic lyrics by Krishna Kanth, "Inthandam" became an instant favorite among music lovers. The song is visually stunning in the film, featuring the charming chemistry between Dulquer Salmaan and Mrunal Thakur in picturesque locations. S. P. Charan's soothing voice adds a magical touch, making this song one of the most loved romantic tracks in recent times.
